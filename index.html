A little about us...

You have better things to do than to watch your grass grow. We quickly and efficiently trim your grass on your schedule. As local small business owners, we like to keep things simple, quality, and financially accessible to everyone. We have crafted such a business model that even your dad would agree that we are affordable. Don't hesitate to reach out to us today for a free quote!

We are a family of 15 employees who have been cutting grass for a collective of over a decade. We bagan as a small, family-owned business and have now spread to all of western North Carolina. Cutting grass may not be glamorous to some, but it is to us! Helping and connecting with others and each other is why we come into work every day.

We hope you are able to get to know us through the exchange of quality work and friendliness. A new customer is a new friend! It is our goal to slowly make the neighborhood a bigger place.
